# tdeck-guides

Practical guides for using the LILYGO® T-Deck / T-Deck Plus as Meshtastic nodes, with a focus on real-world usage, offline maps, troubleshooting and everyday operation.

This repository complements the **mesh-guides** project:

- `mesh-guides`: Meshtastic EU868 guides (PT/EN/ES) for presets, routing roles, network planning and regulation-aware operation in CEPT countries.
- `tdeck-guides`: device-specific documentation for T-Deck / T-Deck Plus (flashing, UI, battery, touchscreen, maps, common issues).

## Documents

- `docs/pt/technical/complete-guide.md` – T-Deck Plus Meshtastic complete guide (PT)
- `docs/en/technical/complete-guide.md` – T-Deck Plus Meshtastic complete guide (EN)
- `docs/es/technical/complete-guide.md` – T-Deck Plus Meshtastic complete guide (ES)
- `docs/ja/technical/complete-guide.md` – T-Deck Plus Meshtastic complete guide (JA, native review recommended)

Additional technical documents (battery, touch, etc.) are also available in PT/EN and may later be expanded to more languages.

## EU868, roles and legal notes

These T-Deck guides do **not** define EU868 regulatory limits or network-wide best practices.

For:

- modem presets and sub-bands,  
- device roles (CLIENT, ROUTER, REPEATER, etc.),  
- conservative range expectations and behaviors to avoid,  

please refer to the **Meshtastic guides** repository:

- <https://github.com/noduscypher/mesh-guides>

Those documents are maintained as the single source of truth for EU/UK 868 MHz operation and basic regulatory context in CEPT countries.
